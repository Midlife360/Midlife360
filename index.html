Midlife360 CIC is a grassroots Social Enterprise serving Cambridgeshire and Peterborough. We represent our community of midlife women in the region, empowering them with the confidence, skills and ability needed to thrive in age-positive work cultures.

We take pride our certification as a Social Enterprise and measure our impact through the women we support, fostering connection, growth and a sense of belonging within our community. 

These diverse, often underestimated, sometimes overlooked women not only enrich our local communities, but also significantly contribute to the boarder fabric of our society.

Our initiative benefits employer and our economy by creating fresh talent pools that were previously inaccessible; a formidable group of midlife women with robust work ethics, sought-after transferable skills, and invaluable life experience.
